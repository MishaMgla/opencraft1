New role mechanics

Choose Pulse, Cross, or Trail before joining. Normal painting charges your role ult; press E once the roster says ready.

  • Pulsepaints a 3 x 3 block centered on you.
  • Crosspaints your tile plus two tiles in each cardinal direction.
  • Trailauto-paints the next 8 distinct tiles you enter.

Hold Space while moving to paint each new tile once. The roster shows every connected player, their role, and ult progress.
